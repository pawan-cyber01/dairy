Our Story

Bringing the purity of traditional dairy to modern homes in Badaun.

Rooted in Tradition

Dairy Garden was born out of a simple desire: to bring unadulterated, farm-fresh dairy products back to the dining tables of Badaun. We noticed a gap between the traditional purity of dairy and the modern need for hygiene and convenience. Our mission is to bridge that gap.

Uncompromising Quality

From the way we source our milk to the final delivery at your doorstep, quality is at the heart of everything we do. Our state-of-the-art processing facility ensures that our Paneer is perfectly soft, our Ghee is rich in aroma, and our Curd is naturally thick—all without the use of artificial preservatives.

Community First

We are proud to serve Badaun City. By focusing on our local community, we ensure that our products spend less time in transit and more time retaining their natural freshness. When you choose Dairy Garden, you're not just choosing premium dairy; you're supporting local farmers and a commitment to health.
